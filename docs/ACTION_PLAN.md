## Time Management

### Weekly Time Allocation

| Activity | Time Investment |
|----------|----------------|
| Content creation (blog/articles) | 3-4 hours |
| LinkedIn posts & engagement | 2-3 hours |
| GitHub wiki writing | 2-3 hours |
| Social media daily engagement | 1-2 hours (15 min/day) |
| Repository updates | 1-2 hours |
| Learning & research | 2 hours |
| **Total** | **11-16 hours/week** |

### Batching Strategy
- **Monday:** Plan week's content, research topics
- **Tuesday/Wednesday:** Write long-form content (blog/wiki)
- **Thursday:** Create social media posts for the week
- **Friday:** Review analytics, engage with community
- **Daily:** 15 min social media, GitHub contributions

## Getting Help

### When You're Stuck

**Content Ideas:**
- Review your recent work - what problems did you solve?
- Check industry news - what's your take?
- Answer a question someone asked you
- Review your learning notes
- Look at trending topics in your field

**Time Management:**
- Reduce frequency rather than stopping
- Focus on one platform at a time
- Repurpose more aggressively
- Batch create content
- Use templates liberally

**Motivation:**
- Review your "why" - career goals
- Celebrate small wins
- Connect with peers doing similar work
- Remember: consistency beats perfection
- Take a break if needed, then return
